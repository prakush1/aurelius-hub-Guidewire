# Aurelius Hub: Strategic Product Vision & Roadmap
### A Framework for AI-Ops Trust in Insurance and Finance
**Vision Lead: Prakush Shende**

## 🎯 The Core Problem: The Enterprise "AI Trust Gap"
As Insurance and Financial platforms migrate to **Olos Cloud Service (Guidewire)** and **SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud**, the complexity of data movement between AI decision engines and core accounting ledgers creates new audit risks.

Traditional Black-Box AI models are insufficient. Businesses need **Explainable & Terminable AI (XAI/TAI)**.

---

## 🏛️ Strategic Pillars
### 1. GRC-First Architecture (Governance, Risk, Compliance)
Aurelius assumes that **drift is inevitable**. By implementing the "Kill Switch" as a first-class feature (not an afterthought), we solve the #1 concern of GRC stakeholders: **"How can we stop the machine if it fails?"**

### 2. Forensic Explainability
Every fraud score or underwriting triage is backed by **Minute-Level Traces**. We use cryptographic hashing (SHA-256) to ensure the data lineage is immutable from Email Ingest to General Ledger reconciliation.

### 3. Integrated Vendor Ecosystem
Aurelius is built to play nicely with:
- **Guidewire Olos**: Policy and Claims transaction data.
- **SAP S/4HANA**: Financial reconciliation.
- **LexisNexis/Hyland**: External verification.
